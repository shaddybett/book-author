## 🎬 Example: ConvertKit Setup (Step by Step)

### **1. Sign Up** (2 min)
- Go to https://convertkit.com
- Click "Get started free"
- Enter email, password
- Verify email
- Choose "Creator" → "Author/Writer"

### **2. Create Form** (5 min)
- Dashboard → "Grow" → "Forms"
- Click "Create New"
- Choose "Inline"
- Pick simple template
- Customize text:
  - Heading: "Join the Story Circle"
  - Button: "Join Now"
- Save & Publish

### **3. Get Form ID** (2 min)
- Click on your form
- Look at URL: `convertkit.com/forms/1234567/edit`
- The number `1234567` is your Form ID
- Copy it!

### **4. Get API Key** (3 min)
- Click your profile (top right)
- Settings → Advanced → API & Webhooks
- Copy "API Secret" (keep this safe!)

### **5. Send Me** (1 min)
Tell me:
- Form ID: `1234567`
- API Secret: `sk_xxxxxxxxxxxxx`

### **6. I Integrate** (10 min)
- I update the code
- Test everything
- Deploy

### **7. You Test** (2 min)
- Go to your website
- Subscribe with your email
- Check inbox for welcome email
- ✅ It works!
