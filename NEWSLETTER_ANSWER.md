## 📋 What Mercy Needs to Do (15 Minutes Total)

### **Step 1: Sign Up** (5 min)
- Go to https://convertkit.com
- Create free account
- Choose "Author/Writer" as category

### **Step 2: Create a Form** (5 min)
- In dashboard, go to "Grow" → "Forms"
- Click "Create New"
- Choose "Inline" form
- Name it "Story Circle"
- Customize and save

### **Step 3: Get Form ID** (2 min)
- Click on your form
- Look at the URL: `convertkit.com/forms/1234567/edit`
- The number `1234567` is your Form ID

### **Step 4: Get API Key** (2 min)
- Settings → Advanced → API & Webhooks
- Copy "API Secret"
