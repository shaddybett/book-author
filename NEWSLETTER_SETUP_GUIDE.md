## 🚀 Quick Setup: ConvertKit (15 minutes)

### **Step 1: Create Account**

1. Go to: https://convertkit.com
2. Click "Get started free"
3. Enter email and create password
4. Choose "Creator" → "Author/Writer"
5. Verify email

### **Step 2: Create Your First Form**

1. In ConvertKit dashboard, go to **"Grow"** → **"Forms"**
2. Click **"Create New"**
3. Choose **"Inline"** form type
4. Select a template (simple one recommended)
5. Customize:
   - Heading: "Join the Story Circle"
   - Description: Your newsletter description
   - Button: "Join Now"
6. **Save & Publish**

### **Step 3: Get Your Form Code**

1. Click **"Embed"** on your form
2. Copy the **JavaScript** code
3. Save the **Form ID** (you'll need this)

**Example Form ID:** `1234567` (found in the URL or embed code)

### **Step 4: Integrate with Website**

I'll show you how below!

### **Step 5: Set Up Welcome Email (Optional but Recommended)**

1. Go to **"Automate"** → **"Sequences"**
2. Click **"New Sequence"**
3. Name it: "Welcome Sequence"
4. Add first email:
   - **Subject:** "Welcome! Here's what to expect..."
   - **Content:** Thank them, introduce yourself, tell them what they'll receive
5. Set trigger: When subscriber joins form
6. **Publish**
