## 🎯 Substack Best Practices

### **Publishing Schedule:**

**Start Simple:**
- Monthly newsletter (very manageable)
- Same day each month (e.g., 1st Monday)
- Consistency matters more than frequency

**As You Grow:**
- Bi-weekly updates
- Special editions for book launches
- Occasional bonus content

### **Content Tips:**

1. **Be Personal** - Readers subscribe for YOU
2. **Tell Stories** - More engaging than facts
3. **Keep It Conversational** - Write like you're talking to a friend
4. **Use Short Paragraphs** - Easier to read
5. **Add Images** - Makes it visually appealing
6. **Include CTAs** - Ask for replies, shares, reviews
7. **End with P.S.** - Often the most-read part!

### **Subject Line Tips:**

✅ Good:
- "What I learned from 10 years of marriage"
- "The truth about immigrating to Canada"
- "Behind the scenes: Writing a memoir"

❌ Avoid:
- "Newsletter #12"
- "Monthly Update"
- "Check this out"

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### **"The Substack page didn't open"**
- Check popup blocker settings
- Use the backup link: "Didn't open? Click here"
- Try different browser

### **"I don't see my subscribers"**
- Go to https://mercylangat.substack.com/publish
- Click "Subscribers" in left menu
- View full list there

### **"How do I write my first post?"**
- Dashboard → "New post"
- Write content
- Click "Publish" at top
- Choose "Send to all subscribers"
